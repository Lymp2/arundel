Working for a fair, free and open society

The Horsham parliamentary constituency is represented in the UK Parliament by John Milne, a member of the Liberal Democrats, who won the seat in the 2024 general election—marking the first time since 1876 that a non-Conservative MP has held the seat. John Milne is a member of the Liberal Democrats and the party's spokesperson for the environment, energy and climate change. He is also the party's spokesperson for the economy, business and trade. He is also the party's spokesperson for the transport, infrastructure and housing. He is also the party's spokesperson for the education, skills and employment. He is also the party's spokesperson for the health and social care. He is also the party's spokesperson for the justice and home affairs. He is also the party's spokesperson for the foreign and commonwealth affairs. He is also the party's spokesperson for the defence and international security.
