This constituency, which lies almost entirely within the South Downs National Park, includes towns and villages such as Arundel, Midhurst, Petworth, Pulborough, Steyning, and Storrington. It is represented in the UK Parliament, while local services are managed by district councils including Arun, Chichester, Horsham, and Mid Sussex.
