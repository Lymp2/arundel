# Councillor David Thompson

**County Councillor for Western Division**

## About David

David has been a County Councillor since 2020 and has extensive experience in civil engineering and infrastructure planning. He's committed to improving the county's roads, bridges, and public facilities.

## Key Priorities

- **Highways**: Improving road conditions and safety
- **Infrastructure**: Supporting major development projects
- **Economic Development**: Attracting investment to the county
- **Environmental Protection**: Ensuring sustainable development
