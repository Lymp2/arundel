# Councillor Emma Wilson

**County Councillor for Central Division**

## About Emma

Emma has been a County Councillor since 2021 and has a background in education and social work. She's passionate about ensuring all residents have access to quality education and support services.

## Key Priorities

- **Education**: Supporting schools and improving educational outcomes
- **Social Services**: Ensuring vulnerable residents get the support they need
- **Highways**: Improving road safety and maintenance
- **Public Transport**: Enhancing bus and rail services
