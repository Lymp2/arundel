# Councillor Sarah Johnson

**District Councillor for Central Ward**

## About Sarah

Sarah has been a District Councillor since 2022 and has lived in the area for over 15 years. She's passionate about environmental protection and community development.

## Key Priorities

- **Environmental Protection**: Working to improve air quality and reduce carbon emissions
- **Community Facilities**: Supporting local sports clubs and community centres
- **Transport**: Improving cycling infrastructure and public transport links
- **Housing**: Ensuring affordable housing for local families
