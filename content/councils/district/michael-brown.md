# Councillor Michael Brown

**District Councillor for North Ward**

## About Michael

Michael has been a District Councillor since 2023 and brings extensive experience in urban planning and community development. He's committed to ensuring sustainable growth while preserving the character of our local area.

## Key Priorities

- **Housing Development**: Supporting affordable housing projects
- **Planning Policy**: Ensuring responsible development
- **Community Safety**: Working with local police and community groups
- **Business Support**: Helping local businesses thrive
