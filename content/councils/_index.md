Our local councillors work tirelessly to represent your interests and make our community a better place to live, work, and raise a family. We have representatives at both District and County Council levels.

## Council Levels

### District Council
District Councillors focus on local services including:
- **Planning and Development** - Housing, commercial development, and local infrastructure
- **Environmental Services** - Waste collection, recycling, and environmental protection
- **Community Facilities** - Parks, leisure centres, and community centres
- **Housing** - Council housing and housing standards

### County Council
County Councillors handle regional services including:
- **Education** - Schools, libraries, and educational support
- **Social Services** - Adult and children's social care
- **Highways** - Major roads, bridges, and transport infrastructure
- **Strategic Planning** - Long-term development and growth

## Get in Touch

All our councillors hold regular surgeries where you can discuss local issues. You can also contact them directly by email or phone. Find their contact details on their individual pages.

## How Councillors Help

Our councillors can assist with:
- **Local Issues** - Problems with council services
- **Planning Concerns** - Development and planning applications
- **Community Projects** - Supporting local initiatives
- **Policy Development** - Shaping council policies and decisions

## Working Together

Our councillors work as a team to ensure the best outcomes for our area. They regularly meet with:
- Local residents and community groups
- Council officers and staff
- Other councillors and political groups
- Local businesses and organizations
