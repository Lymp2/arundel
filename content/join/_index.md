## What We Believe

The Liberal Democrats stand for:
- **Individual Liberty**: Freedom for individuals to live their lives as they choose
- **Social Justice**: A fair society where everyone has the opportunity to succeed
- **Environmental Protection**: Urgent action to tackle climate change
- **International Cooperation**: Working with other nations to solve global problems
- **Democratic Reform**: Making our democracy more representative and accountable

## Membership Options

### Standard Membership
- **£12/year** for adults
- **£6/year** for students and unemployed
- **£1/year** for under 18s
- Full voting rights and benefits

### Joint Membership
- **£20/year** for two people at the same address
- Both members get full benefits
- Ideal for couples and families

### Life Membership
- **£500** one-time payment
- Lifetime membership with no annual fees
- Special recognition and benefits

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I have to attend meetings?
No, membership is flexible. You can be as active or inactive as you wish.

### Can I leave if I change my mind?
Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time with no penalty.

### What if I can't afford the membership fee?
We offer reduced rates for students and unemployed people. Contact us to discuss options.

### Do I have to agree with all party policies?
No, members have different views on various issues. We encourage debate and discussion.

### Can I stand for election as a member?
Yes, members can stand for election as candidates at all levels.
