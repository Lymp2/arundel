# Green Transport Campaign

## Our Vision

We want to make our area a leader in sustainable transport, with safe cycling routes, improved public transport, and better walking infrastructure.

## Why This Matters

- **Climate Change**: Transport is a major contributor to carbon emissions
- **Health**: Active travel improves physical and mental wellbeing
- **Air Quality**: Fewer cars mean cleaner air for everyone
- **Community**: Better transport links bring communities together

## What We're Campaigning For

### 1. Safe Cycling Routes
- Dedicated cycle lanes on main roads
- Off-road cycling paths connecting neighborhoods
- Secure cycle parking at key locations
- Cycle training for children and adults

### 2. Improved Public Transport
- More frequent bus services
- Better integration between different transport modes
- Real-time information displays
- Reduced fares for young people and seniors

### 3. Walking Infrastructure
- Wider, safer pavements
- Better street lighting
- More pedestrian crossings
- Green spaces along walking routes
