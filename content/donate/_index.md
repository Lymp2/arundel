## 💰 **Donation Options**

The official Liberal Democrats donation page offers:

- **One-off donations** from £5 to £500+
- **Regular monthly donations** to provide ongoing support
- **Secure payment** via credit/debit card or Direct Debit
- **Gift Aid** for UK taxpayers (making your donation worth 25% more)
- **Multiple payment methods** including PayPal

## 🎯 **How Your Money is Used**

Every pound you donate helps us:

- **Print campaign materials** to reach more voters
- **Run local events** and community engagement activities
- **Support our candidates** with training and resources
- **Develop policies** that reflect our values
- **Build a stronger** party at all levels

## 🔒 **Security & Compliance**

- **Secure payments** processed by trusted payment providers
- **Electoral law compliance** with all donations properly recorded
- **Data protection** in accordance with our privacy policy
- **Transparency** with proper reporting to the Electoral Commission

## 📞 **Need Help?**

If you have any questions about donating:

- **Phone**: 020 7022 0988 (Monday-Friday, 9:30am-5:30pm)
- **Email**: corporate@libdems.org.uk
- **Large donations**: For donations over £11,180, please contact our fundraising team

## 📋 **Important Information**

- **UK residents only**: Donations over £500 must be from UK residents
- **Own funds**: Donations must be from your own funds, not from third parties
- **Electoral register**: Donations over £500 require verification against the electoral register
- **Public disclosure**: Donations over £11,180 are reported to the Electoral Commission

## 🏠 **Local Party Support**

While this donation goes to the national Liberal Democrats, it helps support our work locally by:

- Providing campaign resources and materials
- Supporting training and development programs
- Enabling national campaigns that benefit our area
- Strengthening the party's overall effectiveness
