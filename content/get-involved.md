There are many ways to get involved with the Liberal Democrats and make a positive difference in our community. Whether you have a few hours a month or want to take on a more active role, we'd love to hear from you.

## Ways to Get Involved

### 🗳️ Campaigning
- **Door-to-door canvassing** - Talk to residents about our policies
- **Phone banking** - Call supporters and members
- **Leaflet delivery** - Help distribute our campaign materials
- **Election day activities** - Support our candidates on polling day

### 📢 Communications
- **Social media** - Help manage our online presence
- **Newsletter writing** - Contribute articles and updates
- **Photography** - Document our events and activities
- **Video production** - Create campaign videos and content

### 🎯 Policy Development
- **Policy forums** - Join discussions about local issues
- **Research** - Help investigate local problems and solutions
- **Consultation responses** - Help shape our responses to council consultations
- **Expert input** - Share your professional knowledge and experience

### 🏛️ Local Government
- **Council monitoring** - Attend council meetings and report back
- **Casework** - Help residents with local issues
- **Committee work** - Support our councillors on specific issues
- **Scrutiny** - Hold the council to account

### 🎉 Events and Social
- **Event organization** - Help plan and run our events
- **Fundraising** - Organize social events and activities
- **Member recruitment** - Help grow our local party
- **Community engagement** - Represent us at local events

## What You'll Get

- **Training and support** - We'll help you develop new skills
- **Friendship** - Meet like-minded people in your area
- **Making a difference** - Help improve your local community
- **Career development** - Gain experience in politics and campaigning
- **Fun** - Enjoy social events and activities

## Time Commitment

We understand that people have busy lives. You can:
- **Give as little as 2-3 hours a month** for basic activities
- **Take on more responsibility** if you have more time
- **Help with specific campaigns** when you're available
- **Join our executive committee** for regular involvement

## Get Started

1. **Contact us** to discuss your interests and availability
2. **Attend a meeting** to meet other members
3. **Try a small task** to see what suits you
4. **Build up your involvement** at your own pace
